Exam Board

Exam Essential

It can help you stay organized. An exam clipboard can help you keep your notes, scratch paper, and answer sheet in one place. This can help you avoid losing track of important information and make it easier to find what you need when you need it. It can help you write more legibly. When you write on a clipboard, your paper is held steady, which can help you write more neatly and legibly. This is especially important if you have shaky hands or if you are writing in a small space.
